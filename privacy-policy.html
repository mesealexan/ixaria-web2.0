Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Who We Are

Ixaria SRL ("Ixaria", "we", "us", "our") is a company based in Timișoara, Romania. We operate the website ixaria.ro and related services. Contact: office@ixaria.ro.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Contact information — name, email address, company name, phone number when you fill in a form or book a call.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, browser type, referring URL, and IP address, collected through analytics tools.
  • Communication data — the content of messages you send us via email or contact forms.
  • Newsletter data — your work email if you opt in to our updates.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and fulfil requests for information or calls.
  • Send you the newsletter you subscribed to (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • Improve our website and services through analytics.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on: (a) your consent where applicable; (b) the performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures; (c) our legitimate interests in running and improving our business; and (d) compliance with legal obligations.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with trusted service providers (e.g. email platforms, analytics tools, calendar booking tools) who process it on our behalf under appropriate data processing agreements. We do not transfer data outside the EEA without adequate safeguards.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above, or as required by law. Newsletter subscribers' data is retained until they unsubscribe.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, request erasure, object to or restrict processing, and data portability. To exercise any right, email office@ixaria.ro. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

8. Cookies

We use cookies to operate and improve the website. See our Cookie Policy for details.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at office@ixaria.ro.
